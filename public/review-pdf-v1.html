VI. Detailed Feedback
Major Issues

1. The statistical methodology needs strengthening. Consider using HLM given the nested structure of organizational data.
2. The limitations section is insufficient. Please address self-selection bias and cross-sector generalizability.

Minor Issues

1. Table 3 formatting needs improvement — column headers are misaligned.
2. Several in-text citations are missing from the reference list (see p. 12, 14).
3. Consider adding a conceptual framework diagram in the introduction.

Comments to Authors

This manuscript addresses a timely and important topic. The multi-sector approach is commendable and adds significant value to the existing literature. With the methodological revisions suggested above, this paper has strong potential for publication.

Confidential Comments to Editor

The manuscript shows promise but requires significant methodological revision. I recommend major revisions with a focus on the statistical analysis approach. The authors should be given the opportunity to resubmit.
